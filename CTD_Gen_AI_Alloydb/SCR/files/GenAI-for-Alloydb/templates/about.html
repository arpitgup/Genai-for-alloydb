About this Demo:

This website is the front-end application for “GenAI for AlloyDB” demo available on go/demos. The demo also includes access to backend resources such as AlloyDB, Notebook instance & source code for Web App on Google Console via the Click-to-Deploy feature/button on the demo page (Note: Available to Argolis users only). Please follow the Demo Guide for the “GenAI for AlloyDB” Demo for steps on how to showcase this demo to your customer.

Search Questions:

You can follow below Questions to Search on Demo section of this App & you will get to experience the answers with generative AI features using pgvector, LangChain and LLMs on Google Cloud.

  1. Do you have a beach toy set that teaches numbers and letters to kids?
  2. Show me the best game to resolve disputes and hard decisions
  3. Show me a good tricycle with canopy basket for kids
  4. Show me a bounce house with castle design
